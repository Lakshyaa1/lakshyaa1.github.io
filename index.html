I’m an Electronics & Telecommunication engineering student interested in building systems that sit at the intersection of hardware and software.

Ongoing Work

UWB-Based Ranging & Localization

Exploring ultra-wideband (UWB) technology for accurate distance measurement and localization using time-based ranging techniques.

The project involves understanding the underlying RF principles, experimenting with ranging methods, and integrating UWB modules into a larger embedded and robotics-oriented system.

Status: Active development and experimentation

FPGA-Based Software Defined Radio

Working on an FPGA-based software defined radio to better understand digital communication systems from the hardware level upward.

This includes implementing and testing signal processing blocks in hardware, studying data paths and timing constraints, and connecting theory from communications courses to real digital implementations.

Status: Active development and experimentation
